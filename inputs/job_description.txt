Senior Backend Engineer — Platform Team

We are looking for a Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform team. You
will design and build high-throughput Python services that power our core
product, partner with infrastructure engineers on AWS-based deployments, and
help scale our data pipelines as the company grows.

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain scalable backend services in Python (FastAPI preferred).
- Design APIs that are performant, observable, and well-tested.
- Own services end-to-end: design, implementation, deployment, and on-call.
- Collaborate on AWS infrastructure (ECS, RDS, S3) and CI/CD via GitHub Actions.
- Improve developer productivity through tooling, testing, and code review.
- Contribute to data pipelines and event-driven systems (Kafka, Airflow).

Requirements:
- 4+ years of backend engineering experience.
- Strong Python expertise; experience with FastAPI or similar async frameworks.
- Hands-on experience with AWS, Docker, and CI/CD.
- Solid SQL and PostgreSQL skills.
- Experience mentoring junior engineers and driving code quality.

Nice to have:
- Experience with Go or another systems language.
- Exposure to Kafka, Airflow, or other streaming/orchestration tooling.
- Background in performance optimization and latency-sensitive systems.
